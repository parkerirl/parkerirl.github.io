Parker Combs is an American musician, composer, producer, and programmer. He's also me! In my free time, I like playing video games, listening to music, watching anime, and studying animation and illustration.

I am currently a programmer at the University of North Dakota School of Medicine and Health Sciences, and I will be graduating with my B.S. in Computer Science in May 2020. I've been producing electronic music since I was a freshman in high school. I play a lot of different instruments (with varying levels of success), but I'm always trying to learn.
